Local Business Showcase

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Local Business Showcase

Proposed Domain: local-biz-showcase.com

I chose this name because it clearly communicates the website's purpose - showcasing local businesses in the community. The name is descriptive, easy to remember, and professional. It immediately tells visitors what they can expect to find on the site. The word "showcase" implies a curated, quality presentation of businesses, which aligns with the goal of highlighting local establishments in an attractive and organized way.

Site Purpose

The Local Business Showcase website serves as a comprehensive digital directory and promotional platform for small businesses in the local community. The site will provide:

  • Business Discovery: A searchable database of local businesses with detailed profiles including photos, descriptions, contact information, and specialties
  • Interactive Features: Search and filter functionality allowing visitors to find businesses by category, location, or services offered
  • Community Support: A platform to promote small businesses that may not have their own websites or strong online presence
  • User-Friendly Navigation: Easy-to-use interface that helps customers connect with local business owners quickly and efficiently

This website aims to strengthen the local economy by increasing visibility for small businesses while providing community members with a convenient way to discover and support local establishments.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: New Resident Looking for Services

"I just moved to this area and need to find a reliable local plumber. What plumbing services are available in my neighborhood, and how can I contact them?"

Scenario 2: Supporting Local Restaurants

"I want to order takeout from a local family-owned restaurant tonight instead of a chain. What local restaurants are in my area, and what type of cuisine do they offer?"

Scenario 3: Finding Specialty Services

"I need a local business that specializes in custom cake decorating for my daughter's birthday. Are there any bakeries or cake decorators in the area, and can I see examples of their work?"

Color Schema

Primary Red (#e74c3c)
Used for: Main headings, call-to-action buttons, business name titles, and important highlights
Secondary Blue (#3c94ce)
Used for: Section headings, navigation elements, links, and interactive elements
Light Gray (#f8f9fa)
Used for: Background sections, card backgrounds, and subtle area divisions
Dark Gray (#838d96)
Used for: Body text, descriptions, and general content for optimal readability

Typography

Poppins Font Family (Headings & Titles)

Usage: Main headings (h1, h2, h3), business names, section titles, and navigation elements. This modern sans-serif font provides excellent readability and a professional appearance that works well for branding.

Open Sans Font Family (Body Text & Content)

Usage: Body text, business descriptions, contact information, and all general content. This highly legible font ensures excellent readability across all devices and screen sizes, making it perfect for the detailed business information that will be displayed.
